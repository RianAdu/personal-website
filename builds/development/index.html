about me

Hi there!
I’m Florian Adu‑Gyamfi, a passionate Front End Developer from Hamburg, Germany who is living in Toronto, Canada.

I use HTML & CSS to turn great designed Photoshop mock‑ups into real live websites and to code fully responsive e‑mail newsletters. My favourite recipe contains a cup of JavaScript with a pinch of jQuery, adding a teaspoon of AJAX to it and mixing it all up with Bootstrap in order to develop highly interactive, responsive web applications and to achieve the best user experience.

Alongside my studies of Media Computer Sciences at the Hamburg University of Applied Sciences in Germany, I was working as an IT‑Support first level agent, which helped me a lot later on. I was able to sharpen my communication skills and gained an insight of how non‑technical people think. Explaining technical terms to non-technical people in an easy way was no problem for me from then on.

I learned the fundamentals of Web Design during my vocational training as a certified Screen Designer. Back then I met the World Wide Web for the first time and instantly fell in love with the Internet and its endless possibilities.

I’m not only a tech nerd, but in my personal life I love watching superhero movies as well as binge-watch one of my favourite TV series. Apart from being interested in movies and cinematography, I like photography a lot. I also really enjoy hanging out with family & friends. I’m a fitness freak who is all about living a healthy lifestyle and in love with basketball.
