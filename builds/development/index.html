Hi My name is Florian Adu-Gyamfi, I’m a passionate Front End Developer from Hamburg, Germany (based in Toronto, Canada).

I first came in contact with the World Wide Web during my vocational training as a certified Screen Designer. Not only did I learn the fundamentals of Web Design, but I also fell in love with the Internet and its endless possibilities. While I was studying Media Computer Sciences at the Hamburg University of Applied Science, I worked as an IT-Support first level agent. This job helped me to sharpen my communication skills and gave me an insight of how non-technical people think. I also learned how to explain technical terms to non-technical people in a simple way.

As a Front End Developer I use HTML & CSS to turn great designed Photoshop mockups into real live websites and to code fully responsive e-mail newsletters. I love to take a cup of JavaScript with a pinch of jQuery, add a teaspoon of AJAX to it and mix it all up with Bootstrap in order to develop highly interactive, responsive web applications and to achieve the best user experience.

In my personal life I’m a tech nerd who loves superhero movies. I enjoy hanging out with my friends and family and binge watch one of my favourite TV Series. I’m interested in movies, cinematography and photography. I love Basketball and I’m a passionate when it comes to fitness and living a healthy lifestyle.
